## Getting Started

### Accessing the CMS

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: `https://yoursite.com/admin`
3. Click "Login with Netlify Identity"
4. Enter your email and password
5. You'll see the CMS dashboard

### First Time Setup

If this is your first time logging in:
1. Check your email for an invitation
2. Click the invitation link
3. Set your password
4. You're ready to go!

## Adding Blog Posts

Blog posts appear on the Blog page and are great for:
- Gardening tips and advice
- Club announcements
- Event recaps
- Member spotlights
- Seasonal guides

### Steps to Create a Blog Post

1. **Log in to the CMS** at `/admin`

2. **Click "Blog Posts"** in the left sidebar

3. **Click "New Blog Post"** button

4. **Fill in the fields:**
   
   - **Title**: The headline of your post (e.g., "5 Tips for Fall Planting")
   
   - **Publish Date**: When the post should appear (click calendar icon)
   
   - **Author**: Your name or "Greenville Garden Club"
   
   - **Excerpt**: A brief summary (1-2 sentences) - this appears in the blog listing
   
   - **Featured Image** (optional): A photo that appears at the top of your post
     - Click "Choose an image"
     - Upload from your computer OR select from previously uploaded images
     - Recommended size: at least 1200x600 pixels
   
   - **Body**: The main content of your post
     - Use the toolbar to format text (bold, italic, lists, etc.)
     - Add headings with the dropdown menu
     - Insert links with the link button
     - Add images inline with the image button

5. **Preview your post:**
   - Click the eye icon to see how it will look

6. **Publish:**
   - Click "Publish" ‚Üí "Publish now"
   - Your post is live!

### Saving Drafts

Not ready to publish?
- Click "Save" instead of "Publish"
- Come back later to finish and publish

## Managing Programs

Programs are your educational classes and events. You can manage programs separately for 2025 and 2026.

### Adding a New Program

1. **Go to the CMS** at `/admin`

2. **Select the year:**
   - Click "2025 Programs" OR
   - Click "2026 Programs"

3. **Click "New [Year] Program"**

4. **Fill in the details:**

   - **Title**: Name of the program (e.g., "Spring Pruning Workshop")
   
   - **Date**: When it's happening (use calendar picker)
   
   - **Time**: Start time (e.g., "7:00 PM" or "2:00 PM - 4:00 PM")
   
   - **Location**: Where it's held (e.g., "Community Center" or "Smith Garden")
   
   - **Description**: Brief overview of the program (2-3 sentences)
     - What will be covered?
     - Who should attend?
     - What to bring?
   
   - **Body** (optional): Additional detailed information

5. **Publish:**
   - Click "Publish" ‚Üí "Publish now"

### Editing an Existing Program

1. Go to "2025 Programs" or "2026 Programs"
2. Click on the program you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Publish" to save

### Deleting a Program

1. Open the program you want to delete
2. Click "Delete" button
3. Confirm deletion

## Editing Pages

Some pages like "History" and "Home" can be edited through the CMS.

### Editing the History Page

1. **Go to CMS** at `/admin`
2. **Click "Pages"** in sidebar
3. **Select "History Page"**
4. **Edit the content:**
   - Update the history text
   - Add new milestones
   - Include photos
5. **Click "Publish"**

### Editing the Home Page

1. **Go to CMS** at `/admin`
2. **Click "Pages"**
3. **Select "Home Page Sections"**
4. **Edit sections:**
   - **Hero Title**: The big headline at the top
   - **Hero Subtitle**: The text below the headline
   - **About Section**: The club description
   - **Featured Message**: Special announcements (optional)
5. **Click "Publish"**

## Working with Images

### Uploading Images

1. Click "Choose an image" in any image field
2. Click "Upload"
3. Select image from your computer
4. Wait for upload to complete
5. Click "Insert"

### Image Tips

- **Size**: Use images at least 1200 pixels wide for best quality
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **File size**: Keep under 2MB for faster loading
- **Content**: Use clear, well-lit photos of gardens, plants, or events

### Finding Images

Don't have photos? Try these free sources:
- Unsplash.com
- Pexels.com
- Pixabay.com

Just make sure to credit photographers if required!

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't log in"

- Make sure you're at the correct URL: `yoursite.com/admin`
- Check your email for the invitation link
- Try resetting your password
- Contact your website administrator

### "My changes aren't showing up"

- Make sure you clicked "Publish" (not just "Save")
- Wait 1-2 minutes for the site to rebuild
- Refresh your browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
- Clear your browser cache

### "I accidentally deleted something"

- Don't panic! 
- Contact your website administrator
- Changes are saved in version control and can be restored

### "The editor isn't loading"

- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Contact your website administrator

### "I need help!"

- Review this guide
- Contact your fellow club officers
- Reach out to your website administrator
- Take screenshots of any errors
