On Saturday, May 2nd, the Greenville Garden Club gathered at the corner of Harris and S. 3rd Street for something we've been doing every first Saturday of May for over 30 years — our Annual Pop Up Plant Sale.
From 8:00 AM to noon, gardeners stopped by to browse a great selection of perennials shared straight from members' own gardens, along with annuals grown from seed. The prices are always friendly, and the plants come with something you can't get at a big box store — the knowledge and care of the gardener who grew them.
![A little chilly, but well spent together](/images/uploads/plantsale%20(3).jpeg)
Every dollar raised goes toward the club's community education and civic beautification efforts, including the flower planters we plant and tend all over Greenville throughout the season. It's a fun raiser in the truest sense of the word.
Thank you to everyone who stopped by, and to the members who dug, potted, and showed up early to make it happen. Here's to 30 more!
